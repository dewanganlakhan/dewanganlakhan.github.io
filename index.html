Who can join the mentorship program:

There are two types of people who can join my program:

  1. People already on a career path (like website development, app development, video editing) but facing challenges:
    • We'll guide and support you in your specific fields.
    • Assist you in troubleshooting and solving problems.
    • Connect you with a mentor who specializes in your area.
    • Help you find real projects so you can start earning.
  2. People interested in an IT career but unsure where to start:
    • Let's learn how to create a website.
    • Learn CSS, HTML, Basic Javascript, and Python for website building.
    • I'll guide you to the best internet resources for fast learning.
    • Share tips and tricks for quick learning and problem-solving.
    • At the end, we'll create a simple dynamic website using what we've learned.

Important note: This is not a classroom program. It's a mentorship where we'll help you find the right courses, videos, and tutorials. You'll need to take your time to practice what you've learned. We'll step in to assist only when you're stuck and unsure how to proceed.

How to join the mentorship program:

People who succeed in life are disciplined and dedicated to their goals and never lose sight of what they want to achieve in life.

To join the mentorship program, here's what you can do:

  1. Follow me on Instagram.
    I'll share links to videos and courses for you to learn from.
  2. Send me a message every day on Instagram/WhatsApp/Telegram for at least 15 days.
    Tell me about what you're learning and how you're progressing.
  3. Based on your progress and dedication, I'll choose you and add you to our WhatsApp group.
    In this group, we'll continue learning and trying new things to reach our goals.
  4. Important Note:
    Even though my mentorship is free and doesn't cost anything, I'd appreciate a kind donation. The money we collect will be used for a good cause to help the public. I'm asking for this so that you take our time and effort seriously, as nobody values things that are completely free.
